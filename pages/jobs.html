We are i.R.L. Games, an independent game company based in Cork, Ireland. We have we have created En-Trance, ShinoBoy, i-SO and most notably Wave Runner. Our current project is a 1st-person science fiction adventure set in a remote corner of the universe. We are a relatively small team of independent developers with a very flat management structure. Our design process consists of iteration combined with a sensible approach to deadlines. We experiment and collaborate with a focus on creating new experiences, and as such we hire people who can help us achieve them.
