About Us

History

Founded in 2018 by game designer Steven Duggan, I.R.L. Games is committed to developing broadly accessible, artistic, thought-provoking and fun experiences. Our work includes titles such as En-Trance, ShinoBoy, i-SO and Wave Runner that have appeared on PC and current gen consoles. Today, we are a team of four creative professionals, coming from a broad range of experience and education. Our hope is to expand the range of experiences possible in video games so that they can be enjoyed and loved by people of all ages, cultures and backgrounds. We are hard at work on bringing our games to more players - and on making new ones.

Studio

Our studio uses a unique approach through experimentation, iteration and collaboration to create an entertaining experience. We take pride in making games that are intutitive and enjoyed by everyone. We are designers, artists, musicians, writers and creators trying to make an impact and positive change with our games. Our team is made up of a mix of diverse talent and experience.

Contact

Due to the high volume of inquiries, we are regrettably unable to answer all of them personally. However, we do read all of the emails we receive, and we will get back to you if we can help.
